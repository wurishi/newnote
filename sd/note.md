环境光照

- 白天黑夜: day / night

- 光环境: sunlight, bright, dark
- 天空: blue sky, starry sky

补充：画幅视角

- 距离: close-up, distant
- 人物比例: full body, upper body
- 观察视角: from above, view of back
- 镜头类型: wide angle, Sony A7 III
